About Meridian Neuro & Spine Institute

For over two decades, Meridian Neuro & Spine Institute has been a trusted destination for complex brain, spine and nerve disorders. Our multidisciplinary team combines deep clinical expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver accurate diagnoses and life-changing treatment outcomes for patients from across the region.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, evidence-based neurological and spinal care that restores mobility, independence and quality of life.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted center of excellence for brain, spine and nerve treatment through innovation and compassion.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity in every diagnosis and recommendation
  • Compassion for patients and their families
  • Excellence through continuous clinical innovation
  • Collaboration across specialties for holistic care
